# Product

## Register

product

## Users
BPHN staff and stakeholders viewing legal aid (Posbankum) analytics. They need to quickly understand the distribution, budget, and performance of legal aid services.

## Product Purpose
To provide clear, actionable insights into the legal aid (Bantuan Hukum) program, tracking budget realization, types of services, and total Posbankum locations across Indonesia.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, clean, data-driven.

## Anti-references
Playful, informal, cluttered, generic SaaS "cream" aesthetics.

## Design Principles
- Clarity over decoration: data is the star.
- High contrast: ensure readability of metrics.
- Professional restraint: maintain an authoritative tone suitable for a government/legal institution.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability of charts and text.
- Clear structural hierarchy.
